What are the humanitarian global issues we are facing right now?

1

Coronavirus Pandemic

Coronavirus Pandemic is afecting right now more than 10 Million People around the world.

2

Poverty

More than 704 Million people in the world live in extreme poverty.

3

Hunger

Around the world, 821 Million people do not have enough of the food they need to live an active, healthy life

4

Water

829 000 people are estimated to die each year from diarrhoea as a result of unsafe drinking-water, sanitation, and hand hygiene.

5

Climate Change

11% of the world’s population is currently vulnerable to climate change impacts such as droughts, floods, heat waves, extreme weather events and sea-level rise.

6

Refugees

At least 79.5 Million people around the world have been forced to flee their homes. Among them are nearly 26 Million refugees, around half of whom are under the age of 18.

7

Gender Equality

Despite progress, gender inequality continues to hold women and girls back and deprive them of basic rights and opportunities.

8

Domestic Violence

About 1 in 3 (35%) of women worldwide have experienced either physical and/or sexual intimate partner violence or non-partner sexual violence in their lifetime.
